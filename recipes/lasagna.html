Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Lasagna pasta squares
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Melt butter and mix it with the milk
  2. Place one lasagna square in a pan
  3. But the meat on the square and some cheese on top of the meat
  4. repeat the previous step until you have 5 layers
  5. Pour the butter and milk mix on top of the last pasta square and trow the last cheese on there as well
  6. place the pan in the oven, 225 Celsius for 45 min
